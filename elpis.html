ELPIS
Elpis, an app designed to help parents of special needs children better manage their kids, from daily diary entries to organising special events to allow their kids to mingle with other special needs children. This app was designed to be a support for young parents with special needs children where they are able to befriend other parents in the same situation so they can share tips on how to better manage their children with other parents. Key features of this app includes Stories section where parents can upload daily stories about their children's day, be it a visit to the therapists or their 4th motor skills training lesson.
